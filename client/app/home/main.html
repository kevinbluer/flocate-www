Record Life's Journey

Plot everywhere you go (and everywhere you've been). Be it a brief trip to the beach or an epic road trip, you'll be able to recall the memory and its exact location in an instant.

Inspire + Be Inspired

Share your favorite locations and trips with the world, or privately with your close friends & family. Be inspired by the weird and wonderful that the world has to offer.

Discover History (and Mystery)

Discover magical places, and epic trips undertaken by the world's legendary explorers. Follow in their footsteps. Like you, we love a good mystery and there's a lot of them out there to discover!
